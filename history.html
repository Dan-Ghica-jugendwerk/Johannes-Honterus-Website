The Rich History of Johannes Honterus School

Early Beginnings

  • 1235: First documentary mention of Brasov
  • 1385: First Brasov citizen enrolled at the University of Vienna
  • 1388: Theodoricus mentioned as the first known school official in Brasov

Johannes Honterus Era

  • 1533: Johannes Honterus returns to Brasov to reorganize education
  • 1539: Publication of first school textbooks printed by Honterus
  • 1543: Establishment of "Constitutio Scholae Coronensis", the first school regulation in our country

Timeline of Major Events

1544:

First mention of a girls' school in the country

1689:

The Great Fire destroys the famous school library

1743-1748:

Reconstruction of the Honterus gymnasium building (now building B)

1903:

The Evangelical Gymnasium in Brasov receives the official name of Honterus Gymnasium

1948:

Through the education reform, the Honterus High School is nationalized

1971:

Establishment of Johannes Honterus General Culture High School

1990:

Johannes Honterus High School becomes a theoretical high school

Recent Developments

In recent years, Johannes Honterus School has continued to evolve and adapt to modern educational needs while preserving its rich heritage. Some notable developments include:

  • Renovation and modernization of school buildings
  • Introduction of advanced technology in classrooms
  • Expansion of international exchange programs
  • Development of new curricula to meet 21st-century educational standards
