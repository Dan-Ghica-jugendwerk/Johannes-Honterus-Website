Clasa Pregatitoare (Preparatory Class)

Our Educational Offer

At Johannes Honterus School, we offer a comprehensive preparatory class program designed to ease the transition of young children into formal schooling. Our curriculum combines traditional subjects with modern, innovative teaching methods to provide a strong foundation for future academic success.

Key Features of Our Preparatory Class

  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Bilingual education (Romanian and German)
  • Introduction to basic literacy and numeracy skills
  • Focus on social and emotional development
  • Creative arts and physical education
  • Regular parent-teacher communication

Daily Schedule

Our preparatory class runs from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM. A typical day includes:

  • Morning circle time
  • Language activities (Romanian and German)
  • Math and logic games
  • Arts and crafts
  • Outdoor play and physical activities
  • Snack time and social interaction

Enrollment Information

To enroll your child in our preparatory class, please contact our admissions office. We accept children who will be 6 years old by September 1st of the academic year.
