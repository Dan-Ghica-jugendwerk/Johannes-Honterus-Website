Projects and Activities

Our Approach to Project-Based Learning

At Johannes Honterus School, we believe in fostering a dynamic learning environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration. Our project-based approach allows students to engage with real-world problems and develop practical skills that will serve them well beyond their school years.

Current Projects

  • Environmental Awareness Campaign: Students are working on a school-wide initiative to reduce waste and promote sustainable practices.
  • Cultural Exchange Program: We have partnered with schools in Germany and Austria for virtual cultural exchange sessions.
  • Community History Project: Upper-class students are documenting the oral histories of local community members.
  • STEM Innovation Challenge: An annual competition where students design solutions to local challenges using science and technology.

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities to support the holistic development of our students:

  • Debate Club
  • Chess Club
  • School Choir and Orchestra
  • Sports Teams (Football, Basketball, Volleyball)
  • Art and Photography Club
  • Robotics Team
  • Model United Nations

Annual Events

  • Science Fair: Held every spring, showcasing student research and experiments.
  • Cultural Festival: A celebration of the diverse cultures represented in our school community.
  • Sports Day: A day of friendly competition and physical activities for all students.
  • Career Week: Inviting professionals from various fields to speak with our students about career opportunities.

Get Involved

We encourage our students to actively participate in these projects and activities. Parents and community members are also welcome to contribute their expertise or support. If you're interested in getting involved, please contact our activities coordinator.
